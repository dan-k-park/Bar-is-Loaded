Bar is Loaded

How-to
  • Enter the desired weight and select your preferred units
  • The gray block at the end of the weights represents a 2.5kg/5.5lb collar
  • The guide on the left gives the combination of weight plates required to meet the given load per side
  • You may toggle freely between lbs and kg
  • Some inputs may be rounded up/down to the nearest possible lb/kg conversion
  • The minimum load is 25kg (20kg empty bar + 2.5kg collar pair)
